What is treeative writing?

A series of four tree-themed online writing workshops.

Each can be booked individually, with a sliding price scale.

A few important principles:

* Trees are the starting point but you can branch out - all my

writing workshops encourage improvisation and seeing where the

story takes you.

* The tree meditation is personal to you; in all the other

workshops we share our work so we get a mini storytelling circle.

* This means the vibe is supportive and suitable for writers at all

levels of confidence and ability. I love to hear

how everyone has responded to the same suggestion.

* Writing exercises are short, encouraging spontaneity.
